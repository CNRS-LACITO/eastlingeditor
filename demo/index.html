What is EASTLing ?

EASTLing : Easy Annotation & Synchronization Tool for Linguists
Web-based application for linguists that has been designed to :
create documents with attached audio recordings and pictures (manuscript pages)
input metadata and annotations (transcriptions, translations, comments)
synchronize annotations along with audio recording and picture attached to the document
display documents in a specific player that plays the audio recording, shows the words being read in the picture and highlights related annotations in a real-time process.
developed by Matthew DEO (Web programmer) in the scope of the DOREMIFA project (lead by Alexis MICHAUD - MICA Hanoi/LACITO/CNRS)
